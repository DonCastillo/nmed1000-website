About Me

Hi. My name is Don Castillo. I'm currently pursuing my second degree as a Computer Science major at the University of Lethbridge. I also work as a contract front-end developer at Tangle Media where I design and implement website interfaces.

Why Computer Science if you might ask? I just love to code, design, and make something that can be useful. I realized my passion to coding when I first got into an HTML class when I was a freshman highschool.

Knowledge is a continuous process. I believe that perseverance and dedication can turn into a sweet success. My goal after university is to pursue a career in software or web development. I also have interest in pursuing a Master's degree in a different field other than Computer Science.

Thank you for visiting my personal page. Take a look on what's in store on my website.

Why U of L?

It was seven years ago when my parents brought up the idea of me attending the University of Lethbridge. During that time, the family just settled here in Canada from the Philippines. I was then a second-year student of Development Communication, my first degree. I went back to the Philippines to finish the last two years of my program. I came back to Canada in 2015, worked in different jobs before making a career change to information technology. I attended Lethbridge College from 2017 to 2019, then I decided to attend as a post diploma undergraduate student at U of L.

What I like about U of L is the endless opportunities that it can give to its students. Aside from a more theoretical and practical learning and experience that I can get, and the diverse people that I can meet and build career networks with, my primary goal is to get a job related to my education through co-op. Co-op helps students build a career foundation in their chosen field as early as their first year at U of L. The university has wide range of employer network which is important in helping students with their co-op placement.

Combined with academic and employment background, I am sure that students graduating from U of L can land a successful career in the future.
